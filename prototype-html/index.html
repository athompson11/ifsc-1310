A general biography

“History only remembers the victors. Stand with Noxus, and be remembered forever.”
-Darius

Darius' began life as an orphan in the harsh and impoverished city of Basilich with his brother, Draven, something that would come to shape him for the rest of his life. He was a born fighter and struggled to provide for himself and his brother. When he became a teenager, Basilich was siezed by Cyrus, who commanded a Warhost for the Noxian empire - and he was taken in by Cyrus, who took a liking to him. From his teens to his adulthood, he fought alongside Cyrus with his brother Draven, and the trio would go on to conquer a path from one end of the continent to the other, proving the pair as a mighty duo, and elevating them within Noxian society. You see, within Noxus, any person can rise to status and power, even command their own warband, irregardless of who they were or where they were from - no titles were inherited, no land was bought, or sold, the mighty took the place at the helm on their own. Darius took this ideology and fervently worked to grow from a soldier in Cyrus' warband to commanding officer and eventually to general, which he earned through taking the head of a Noxian general who had ordered a retreat - and reforming broken lines to win the battle of Dalamor.

When it was heard that the mighty Darius has gotten his own command, he attracted recruits from far and wide - and many, he turned away, only accepting the most disciplined, iron willed, and strongest of them all. He - and his warband - quickly gained notoriety, and it became common for many cities to surrender at the first site of his banners, even in lands far beyond Noxus. After a hard fought victory against the mountain fortresses of Varju, ending in a year the resistance of a people who had for decades resisted Noxian aggression - Darius was named the Hand of Noxus by the Emperor himself. All that knew him, and of him, knew that he did not want power or adulation - just for Noxian victory over all, and to help the oppressed rise against their weak oppressors. When taking a city ruled by a petty king who was well known to rule by bloodshed, he said: Kings demand you kneel before their heirlooms and unearned titles. Noxus wants you to stand, so that you can stand in the glory shared by all.

His role in the game

Role
Juggernaut
As a Juggernaut champion, Darius is a close range brawler and is best suited to ambush or direct melee engagements where he can take advantage of his passive bleed-on-hit and his abilities.
Preferred Lane
Top
As a close range fighter that is also tanky, Darius needs a solo lane to be able to easily farm enough gold to snowball him into either a tank role or a damage role late game.

In general, Darius tends to scale very well, whether he's built to take punishment or dish it out, and brings the pain with his bleed passive.

Abilities

  1. Passive: Hemorrhage

    Darius' attacks and damaging abilities cause enemies to bleed for physical damage over 5 seconds, stacking up to 5 times. Darius enrages and gains massive Attack Damage when his target reaches max stacks.

    This is what makes him truly unique - all of his abilities cause a lasting impact in terms of damage and bleed can and will kill your opponents for you.
  2. Q: Decimate

    Darius winds up and swings his axe in a wide circle. Enemies struck by the blade take more damage than those struck by the handle. Darius heals based on enemy champions and large monsters hit by the blade.

    Darius' Q is an amazing poke, a great follow up to his E as enemies are trying to flee, and has a healing element that gives him a bit of sustain.
  3. W: Crippling Strike

    Darius's next attack strikes an enemy's crucial artery. As they bleed out, their Move Speed is slowed.

    This is a great forced engagement tool - you hook an enemy, then stop the coward from fleeing you as you unleash your full might on them.
  4. E: Apprehend

    Darius hones his axe, passively causing his physical damage to ignore a percentage of his target's Armor. When activated, Darius sweeps up his enemies with his axe's hook and pulls them to him.

    This is Darius' engagement method, and is a two-fer - his passive causes him to ignore a large percentage of his opponents armor so that none may hide from him, and his E drags a ranged opponent or a fleeing opponent to him.
  5. R: Noxian Guillotine

    Darius' attacks and damaging abilities cause enemies to bleed for physical damage over 5 seconds, stacking up to 5 times. Darius enrages and gains massive Attack Damage when his target reaches max stacks.

    Darius' axe spares no one, and if you kill someone with the true damage of this ability, you can kill someone else.
